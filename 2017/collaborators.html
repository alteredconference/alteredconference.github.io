Be a Part of Altered 2017

Call for Collaborators

Our aim is to provide a platform to talk, to share, to open up and give something from your heart - present a talk, lead a workshop or ritual, help to market and promote the conference, secure sponsorship or join us in planning and organizing the event - we need your help and collaboration!

  • Anyone who has worked through crisis from altered states of mind
  • Those who have overcome illness, addiction or crisis using psychedelics
  • Researchers, therapists, doctors, somatic practicioners
  • Herbalists, bodyworkers, healers
  • Workshop facilitators, artists
  • Shamans, ritualists, psychonauts
  • Marketers, promotors, grant writers, social network masters

Subjects of Interest

  • Various models of understanding and interacting with crisis
    • astrological
    • Jungian/alchemical
    • artistic and/or movement-based
    • massage and body work
    • ritual (psychedelic, cacao, magickal or otherwise)
    • plant intelligence and Gaia
    • permaculture and natural systems
  • Therapeutic use of psychedelics, entheogens and empathogens
  • Sober parties
  • Sensory deprivation and/or flotation tanks
  • Fasting, solo travel, monastic retreats
  • Ritual, magickal and tantric sex and/or celibacy
  • Polyamory, non-standard/non-monogomous relationships

Call for Volunteers

Volunteers have the opportunity to get involved with us, join our community, and take part in creating something of meaning. Volunteers also get team perks like complimentary passes to the conference and/or related events.

Tasks include location setup and teardown, hanging posters and distributing promotional materials, helping with tech/sound, collecting tickets and helping out during the event, etc...

Important Dates

  • Application deadline for talks/workshops/rituals: July 30, 2017
  • Decision: August 30, 2017
  • Application deadline for volunteers: September 30, 2017
