Donations

100% of donations will go to putting on Altered Conference 2017 - paying for the venue, speakers and staff, renting equipment, and helping to sponsor those who wish to come but wouldn't be able to for financial reasons.

We're committed to fairly compensating everyone putting their time and talent into creating Altered Conference and we're working hard to find sponsors to help offset the costs. If you can't come but would like to support what we're doing or just want to help out financially, please consider donating.
