A conference exploring altered states of consciousness

November 2017 / Berlin

Objectives

  • To bring together like-minded people who might not ordinarily interact
  • To create an open atmosphere to discover, share and consider our relationships to each other and our world
  • To discuss harm reduction for individuals, communities, cultures and our planet
  • To provide access to stimulating workshops, rituals and lectures
  • To facilitate the building of postive creative relationships and collaborations
  • To build a landing zone - a place where the dreams we encounter in altered states can be invoked and given the energy required to manifest
