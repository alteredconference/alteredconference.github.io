Crisis, Like Never Before

We're happy to officially announce Altered Conference 2017!

The call for papers is open and we are accepting proposals for talks and workshops surrounding altered states of consciousness, specifically those related to this year's theme of crisis.

The world is roiling - that is nothing new - but like never before each of us are forced to watch it unravel in gory detail via mass media, social networking and the internet.

Like never before we are connected to each other and therefore, like never before, we are affected by each other. This deep sympathetic relationship has always been there, but with increased awareness so too does its power increase.

The world is in crisis like never before but we can alter our relationship to it, and to each other, and instead of crisis, see opportunity. Things are unstable, but it means change is possible like never before.

Our aim is to provide a platform to talk, to share, to open up and give something from your heart. A lecture, a workshop, a ritual, a connection... We need your help and collaboration. We're looking for the following people:

  • those who have overcome illness, addiction or crisis using psychedelics
  • researcher, therapists, doctors, somatic practicioners
  • herbalists, bodyworkers, healers
  • workshop facilitators, artists
  • shamans, psychonauts

Our Interests

  • Various models of understanding and interacting with crisis
    • Astrological
    • Jungian/Alchemical
    • Artistic and/or Movement-Based
    • Massage and Body Work
  • Sober parties and group ritual
  • Psychedelic group ritual
  • Therapeutic use of psychedelics, entheogens and empathogens

Inpiration

For me, personally, this has been the most crisis-filled year of my life - and when I look around me, I see many, if not most, of the people in my life going through crises of their own. Big, small, it doesn't matter - there's a beautiful quote from Viktor Frankl that comes to mind:

...suffering is similar to the behavior of gas. If a certain quantity of gas is pumped into an empty chamber, it will fill the chamber completely and evenly, no matter how big the chamber. Thus suffering completely fills the human soul and conscious mind, no matter whether the suffering is great or little.

It's easy to get lost in the fog, to wander in circles, to give up. I've seen the people around me struggling, but like never before I've seen people facing their struggles, accepting their fears, digging in and fighting hard. Not giving in, not taking the easy way out. I've been inspired by the people who stop ignoring problems, who suffer consciously, and accept that suffering as the true joy of life.
