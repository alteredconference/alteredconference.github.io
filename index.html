Call for Papers

We're happy to officially announce Altered Conference 2017

The call for papers is open and we are accepting proposals for talks and workshops surrounding altered states of consciousness, specifically those related to this year's theme:

crisis, like never before

The world is roiling, and perhaps that is nothing new, but like never before each of us are forced to watch it unravel in gory detail via mass media, social networking and the internet.

Like never before we are connected to each other and therefore, like never before, we are affected by each other. By accepting depression and fear we pull others down with us, but we can also help to boost each other up.

We can alter our perception of crisis and instead see opportunity. Things are unstable, but it means change is possible like never before.

Our aim is to provide a platform for [ who? ] to do [ what? ]

Areas of Interest

  • Various models of understanding and interacting with crisis
    • Astrological
    • Jungian/Alchemical
    • ...
  • Psychedelics as part of modern ritual
  • Therapeutic use of psychedelics
  • Group work, healing, medical assistance
